About Us

Pacific Trails Resort believes everyone deserves a place to pause, breathe, and enjoy the beauty of nature. Our goal is to create a relaxing escape where guests can step away from busy schedules and experience the peaceful rhythm of the coast. Here, the sound of the ocean, the fresh air, and the scenic trails invite you to slow down and truly enjoy the moment. Whether you're looking for adventure or quiet time, Pacific Trails offers something for everyone.

About Our Team

Pacific Trails Resort's team truly loves nature and believes it should be shared. Many of us are hikers, explorers, and outdoor enthusiasts ourselves, so helping guests experience the beauty of the coast is something we genuinely enjoy. We work together to make sure every guest feels supported, safe, and ready to explore. Whether you need guidance for your next adventure or simply want a quiet place to relax, our team is always nearby and happy to help make your stay memorable.

What Makes Us Special:

  • Nature at your doorstep.
  • Local flavors and good meals.
  • Comfort and adventure.
  • A place to reconnect.
  • Different ways to explore.
